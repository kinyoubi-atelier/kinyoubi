# Kinyoubi Atelier & Co. — Legal Templates

This directory contains template legal documents for engagements with Kinyoubi Atelier & Co.

## Document Index

### Data Processing Addendum (DPA.md)
**For:** Enterprise buyers and regulated industries requiring GDPR/DPDP compliance proof before signing an MSA.

Covers:
- Processor obligations under GDPR Article 28 and DPDP Act 2023
- Subject matter, duration, nature, purpose, and categories of processing
- Security controls (TLS 1.2+, encryption at rest, access controls, logging)
- Sub-processor notification and objection windows
- International data transfer references (Standard Contractual Clauses)
- Breach notification (72 hours)
- Audit and inspection rights
- Data subject rights assistance
- Liability and governing law references to MSA

**Status:** Template v1 — DRAFT. Not counsel-reviewed. Do not execute without licensed legal review in both India and the relevant counterparty jurisdiction.

---

### Standard Contractual Clauses – Module Two (SCC-Module-2.md)
**For:** Any engagement involving transfer of EU personal data outside the EEA.

Covers:
- EU Commission Decision 2021/914 — Module Two (Controller to Processor) structure
- Parties and competent supervisory authority
- Description of transfer and processing
- Technical and organizational measures (TOMs)
- Sub-processor list and management
- Incorporation by reference into the main DPA

**Status:** Template v1 — DRAFT. Not counsel-reviewed. Do not execute without licensed legal review in both EU and India.

This document must be executed as a standalone instrument alongside the DPA for EU data transfers. It is incorporated by reference in the DPA but does not replace a separately signed SCC.

### Master Services Agreement (MSA.md)
**For:** The umbrella agreement governing all services and engagements. Required reading for enterprise legal teams before contract signature.

Covers:
- Parties and definitions
- Services (by reference to Statement of Work)
- Fees and payment (Net 30 default; late interest; currency: USD/EUR/GBP/INR)
- Intellectual property (work product assigned on payment; background IP retained; open-source carve-outs)
- Confidentiality (mutual, 3-year term)
- Warranties (professional-standard services; no implied warranties)
- Limitation of liability (cap: fees paid in prior 12 months; standard carveouts)
- Indemnification (mutual; IP indemnity from Kinyoubi; tax indemnity standard)
- Data protection (DPA incorporated by reference)
- Force majeure
- Termination (for convenience, for cause, effects)
- Notices and assignment
- Entire agreement and severability
- Governing law: India (Arbitration and Conciliation Act 1996), with optional SIAC Singapore seat for enterprise counterparties

**Status:** Template v1 — DRAFT. Not counsel-reviewed. Do not execute without licensed legal review in both India and the relevant counterparty jurisdiction.

### Statement of Work Template (SOW-TEMPLATE.md)
**For:** Each discrete engagement or project. Supplements the MSA with scope, schedule, acceptance criteria, and project-specific fees.

Covers:
- Parties and effective date
- Description of services
- Deliverables and milestones
- Acceptance criteria
- Fees (fixed, T&M, or hybrid)
- Schedule and timeline
- Change control
- Project-specific assumptions
- Client responsibilities
- IP carveouts specific to the engagement

**Status:** Template v1 — DRAFT. Not counsel-reviewed. Do not execute without licensed legal review in both India and the relevant counterparty jurisdiction.

## Who to Send These To

- **Enterprise legal teams:** DPA, MSA, and (if applicable) SCC Module Two
- **Project managers and clients:** Statement of Work (SOW) for each engagement
- **Security/compliance questionnaires:** Refer to the published `/security` page and the DPA summary on `/legal/dpa`

## Important Notes

- **Not counsel-reviewed:** These templates are drafts created by Kinyoubi Atelier & Co. and have not been reviewed by a licensed attorney. They must be reviewed by counsel in both India and the relevant counterparty jurisdiction before execution.
- **Customization required:** Each engagement will have specific terms, deliverables, fees, and IP carveouts. These templates are starting points, not final agreements.
- **Data subject to change:** All terms, especially liability caps, insurance commitments, and sub-processor lists, are subject to negotiation and must be confirmed in writing before execution.
